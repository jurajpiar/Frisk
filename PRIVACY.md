# Frisk Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 19 July 2026**

Frisk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- Frisk has no analytics, no telemetry, and no advertising.
- Frisk creates no user accounts and requires none.
- Archives you open are read locally on your Mac. Their contents never leave your device.
- Frisk makes no network requests. The App Store version contains no update mechanism; the direct-download version checks a signed update feed (Sparkle) that transmits no personal information.

Because Frisk collects nothing, there is nothing to share, sell, or delete.

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at
<https://codeberg.org/deadkittens/Frisk/issues>.
